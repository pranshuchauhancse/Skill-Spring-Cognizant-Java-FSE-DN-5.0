Community News Bulletin

The Community Development Committee has announced several exciting events for this month. Residents are encouraged to participate in tree plantation drives, health awareness camps, sports competitions, and cultural programs. These activities aim to promote community involvement and strengthen relationships among local residents. Volunteers are welcome to support event management and help make each program successful. Citizens are also invited to share their ideas for future community projects and activities. Everyone is encouraged to work together to build a cleaner, safer, and more active neighborhood.
